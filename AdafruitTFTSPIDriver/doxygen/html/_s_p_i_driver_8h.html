Initializes the SPI hardware to operate in the Master mode, to send the most significant bit first, and to have slave clock frequency of SOME Hz. Initializes the ss, sclk, and mosi pins as outputs and the miso pin as input. It also drives the mosi and sclk pins low and the ss pin high.
